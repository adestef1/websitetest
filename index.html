What is BOP?

Have you ever wondered what Brown University students actually think about today’s pressing issues, both at Brown and in the world? Brown Opinion Project (BOP), a new club this semester, seeks to conduct frequent representative opinion polling of the Brown community on topics ranging from the University’s Covid-19 policies to political issues such as campaign finance reform and gun ownership laws. We hope that the results of these polls will help inform University decision-making and contribute to meaningful discourse on campus.
